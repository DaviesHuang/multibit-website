MultiBit Bounties

The following bounties are now closed:

  • Farsi translation of MultiBit

  • Arabic translation of MultiBit

  • Hindi translation of MultiBit


The bounties all have the same overall structure:

  1. People pledge BTC for the translation of MultiBit into a particular language.

  2. Interested translators perform the translation on translate.multibit.org.

  3. After quality checking, the terms that are approved (and hence will appear in MultiBit) are paid for from the bounty for that language. Payment is made to the translator pro rata on the number of phrases translated.

Details for the individual bounties are given below. The general terms and conditions for all the bounties are given in the "Terms and Conditions for all bounties" section.

Terms and conditions for all bounties

Terms: For each language the bounty will be paid to translators of the untranslated terms at the MultiBit translation site.

Start and end: The bounty starts on the specified start date and ends when there are 0 terms to translate or the end of specified end date, whichever comes sooner.

Payment: Payment from the bounty collected at the address specified will be paid by Jim within 7 days of the end of the bounty period. It will be paid pro rata to translators, based on the number of terms that get approved (and hence get into MultiBit itself). Translators will be contacted via the translate.multibit.org messaging system to obtain Bitcoin addresses for payment.

Small print:

  1. If the bounty is not claimed, BTC will be returned within 7 days of the bounty end to the address of the first transaction input of the sending transaction. (This means you should send it from an address you definitely control i.e. no green addresses).

  2. Any BTC sent to the bounty address after the end of the bounty will be transferred to the general MultiBit donation address.
