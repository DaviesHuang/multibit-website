What can MultiBit do ?

Here is a short summary of MultiBit's features:

  • Quick and easy to install
  • All private keys are kept encrypted on your local machine (or on a USB stick)
  • Blockchain synchronization is much faster than the reference client (usually a minute or so)
  • The stored data is much smaller (usually a few megabytes)
  • Multiple wallets (curent account, savings account, expenses etc)
  • Secure delete of wallets (prevents malicious recovery if you sell your computer)
  • Multiple languages (25+ at the last release)
