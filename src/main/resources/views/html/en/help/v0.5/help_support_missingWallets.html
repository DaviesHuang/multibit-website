I opened MultiBit and my wallets were missing


What are the symptoms ?

You open MultiBit and instead of seeing your wallet(s) in the Wallet list, you don't see anything.

What can you do when this happens ?

Most likely you will still have your wallet(s) on your disk.

In the 'Help | Help Contents' menu option there is a section called Troubleshooting. This describes where MultiBit stores the default wallet (multibit.wallet).

Have a look in that directory. You can open any wallet there using the 'File | Open Wallet' option.

If you created a new wallet and stored it in another directory then you will have to look there.
