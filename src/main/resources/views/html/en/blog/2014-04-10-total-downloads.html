In the very early days MultiBit was only used by the bitcoinj mailing list. We don't have records from then so I have put the downloads to be 'tiny'. They were probably less than a 100 a month.

Here is the data as a chart:

You can clearly see the Bitcoin booms of April 2013 and November 2013. The bump in the fall of 2013 was caused by MultiBit becoming the recommended desktop client on bitcoin.org, which doubled downloads.

Currently Gary and I are working hard on the next version of MultiBit (MultiBit HD) which is targeted for mainstream Bitcoin users. It will be fascinating to see what this chart looks like in 12 months time.
